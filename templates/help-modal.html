Kevscript Editor:

Action Shortcut
Start searching Ctrl-F / Cmd-F
Find next Ctrl-G / Cmd-G
Find previous Shift-Ctrl-G / Shift-Cmd-G
Replace Shift-Ctrl-F / Cmd-Option-F
Replace all Shift-Ctrl-R / Shift-Cmd-Option-F

Tips:

You can move the whole editor stage by drag'n'dropping the grey background.

You can also zoom in and out, by using your mouse wheel.

Double-clicking the grey background will reset "move" and "zoom" parameters to default.
